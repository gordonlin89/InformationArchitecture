Undergraduate Admissions

For all full-time higher education courses at universities and colleges in the UK, students must apply online via UCAS. There are three types of applicant. For all applications, there are full instructions on the UCAS website to make it as easy as possible for you to fill in your online application, plus help text where appropriate. Please note that your personal statement must be written in your own words.
